Resolving Conflicts

As you work, conflicts may arise because of operations you have performed. The Conflicts tab suggests ways to resolve these conflicts. For example, if a class has a slot that references a class that currently does not exist in the working project, this will show up as a conflict. When there are conflicts, the tab name is highlighted in red. 

To resolve a conflict:

  1. Click on the conflict to select it. You can only resolve one conflict at a time.
  2. If more than one solution is shown at the bottom of the Conflicts tab, click the desired solution to select it.
  3. Click Do It to perform the operation.

Based on your knowledge of the ontolgie(s), you may decide to resolve the conflict in some other way. For example, you can create your own operation to resolve the conflict.
