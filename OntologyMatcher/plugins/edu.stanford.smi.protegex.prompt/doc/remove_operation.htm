Removing a Suggested Operation

For merge and moving frames modes, suggested operations are shown on the To Do list at the Suggestions tab. You can remove operations from this list as follows:

  1. Highlight the operation(s) you want to remove in the To Do list. To highlight more than one operation, hold down the Ctrl key or the Shift key as you click.
  2. Click the Remove button at the top right of the To Do list.
    The highlighted operation(s) are removed from the list.
