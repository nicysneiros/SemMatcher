UMLS Tab

The UMLS tab allows you to search the National Library of Medicine's Unified Medical Language System (UMLS) and annotate your current Protégé ontology with terms, concept ids, synonyms, relations, and other information from the UMLS.

Authors: Qi Li & Philip Shilane
Current Contact: Jennifer Vendetti
Institution: Stanford University

Level of Support: This plug-in is no longer under active development and we are not considering feature requests at this time. We will answer questions about the plug-in via the protege-discussion mailing list and also try to fix bugs as time permits.
